Step 04
Screen / Display Setup — THX Guidelines
THX recommends a viewing angle of 26°–36° from the primary seating position for an immersive experience, with 40° as the outer maximum.
Display Type
THX Recommended Screen Size
Based on your seating distance from Room & Seating (Step 02). THX optimal range = 30° viewing angle (sweet spot), acceptable range 26°–36°.
Screen Wall Placement
Screen center should align close to ear height of the MLP, per THX guidance, especially for front-row seating.
